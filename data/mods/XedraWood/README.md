XedraWood: Stone and Sorcery

This mod acts as an expansion for using Xedra Evolved and Innawoods together, developing on the basic compatibility performed by the base game.

1. Allow researching and gaining hedge magic Innawoods--deduction is renamed "occult" to fit the low-tech aesthetic.
2. Reworks some hedge magic descriptions and ingredients for a more low-tech feel
3. Adds groups of magical plants throughout the wilderness
4. Adds new monsters, like dragon-birds, ghosts at the crossroads, mi-go psychics and triffid mages, and mysterious lights in the forest.
5. Adds new locations, like the Cathedral of Flesh, the ruined roads, or lone towers.
