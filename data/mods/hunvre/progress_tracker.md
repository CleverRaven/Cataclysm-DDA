# Progress Tracker

This document is used to track progress on planned content for Hunvre, **it holds no coercive value by itself**, if I or anyone else wants to add anything to the mod that isn't in the list below they can, this is merely to give players and possible collaborators an idea of what to expect to come to the mod and when.

I divided the list in high/medium/low priority segments, I could have also used a short/medium/long term format but I want to make it clear that low priority features are not things that are necessarily difficult or time intensive to add, just that I consider they're not needed in priority. **It is in fact extremely probable that I work on low priority content just because I find it more enjoyable or need a break from other projects.**

What I consider high priority at the moment is **content that "opens up" design spaces and narrative possibilities**, for example I consider industrial type maps to be high priority because they're both important to the narrative, missing from the current pool of maps entirely and can be used to spawn more types of items, different monsters etc. 

Medium priority is content that doesn't necessarily open up a lot of design space but has some importance and/or doesn't exist at all yet.

Low priority content is stuff that already exist in some way but could be improved or added to.

The "May get added" segment is stuff that I'm thinking about but either I'm not sure it's a good idea yet or I think it's a good idea but I don't know how it should work yet.
